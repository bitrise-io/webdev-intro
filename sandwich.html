Vegan Turkey Club sandwhich (Classic)

  1. Buy ingredients
    • A cucumber
    • Another cucumber
    • Yet another cucumber
  2. Slice the cucumber in two halves
  3. Slice the other cucumber into small slices
  4. Grate the third cucumber
  5. Put the cucumber slices between the cucumber buns
  6. Spice it up with some grated cucumber from step
  7. Post it on social media
    1. Facebook
    2. Instagram
    3. Diaspora
